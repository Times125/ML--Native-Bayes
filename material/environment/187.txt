Many of us have no problem eating rabbits, so the greys could be a cheap addition to our diet. The bird population would also benefit from a cull of these pests. My grandmother, originally from Saddleworth, would make a  Nelson One-Roll  when she had a small piece of pastry left over   a circle of pastry folded over some currants and a little bit of sugar, the edges crimped then baked.
